## Sample QA Note

Source copy:

> Flat monthly plans with monthly effective-word limits. Translate unlimited documents within your plan allowance, with no per-character fees.

Korean-market wording:

> 월 정액으로 제공되는 유효 단어량 안에서 문서를 개수 제한 없이 번역할 수 있습니다. 글자 수 기반 추가 과금은 없습니다.

Quality note:

I avoided translating "unlimited documents" as unlimited usage, because the page also mentions monthly effective-word limits. The Korean wording keeps the pricing promise clear without overstating the plan.

## Example Report Style

Title: Pricing copy uses inconsistent unit language across public landing page

Observed:

- The page uses "flat monthly", "per-word monthly", "effective words/month", and "unlimited documents" in related pricing explanations.

Expected:

- The pricing unit should be described consistently, with document-count benefits explained separately.

Impact:

- Medium. This may reduce trust for first-time buyers comparing translation tools, especially in a localized Korean sales flow.
